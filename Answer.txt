## Part A: Network Devices

### Router
Purpose: Connects different networks.
How it Works: Routes data packets between networks.
Usage: Home Wi-Fi routers.

### Switch
Purpose: Connects devices in a LAN.
How it Works: Uses MAC addresses.
Usage: Offices and schools.

### Hub
Purpose: Connects devices.
How it Works: Broadcasts data to all ports.
Usage: Older networks.

### Access Point
Purpose: Provides Wi-Fi access.
How it Works: Connects wireless devices to wired networks.
Usage: Offices, cafes, homes.

### Firewall
Purpose: Protects networks.
How it Works: Filters network traffic.
Usage: Home and enterprise security.

### Modem
Purpose: Connects to ISP.
How it Works: Converts signals.
Usage: Internet connection.
